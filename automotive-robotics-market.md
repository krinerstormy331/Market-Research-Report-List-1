<p><p>The automotive robotics market encompasses various robot types tailored for manufacturing processes. Articulated robots feature rotating joints, offering flexibility for complex tasks. Cartesian robots operate on three linear axes, ideal for precise and repetitive tasks. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear motion, suited for assembly and material handling. SCARA robots are known for their high speed and precision in horizontal movements, perfect for assembly tasks. Other types include Delta and collaborative robots, addressing specific needs in automotive production.</p></p>
