<p><p>The Automated Refuse Collection System market comprises two primary types: the Gravity System and the Full Vacuum System. The Gravity System relies on gravity to facilitate the movement of waste through underground networks, effectively minimizing the surface footprint. In contrast, the Full Vacuum System employs vacuum technology to transport waste through sealed pipes, ensuring efficiency and odor control. Both systems are designed to enhance urban waste management by increasing operational efficiency, reducing labor costs, and improving environmental sustainability.</p></p>
