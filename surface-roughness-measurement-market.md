<p><strong>The Surface Roughness Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Noncontact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface roughness measurement market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact methods. Contact measurement involves physically touching the surface with a probe to assess roughness by tracing its profile, offering high accuracy for various materials. Non-contact methods utilize optical techniques, such as laser scanning or interferometry, to measure surface roughness without physical contact, making them suitable for delicate or soft materials. Both methods are essential in industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and electronics for quality control.</p></p>
