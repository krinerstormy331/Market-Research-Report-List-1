<p><p>Automotive On-Board Diagnostics (OBD) is a system integrated into vehicles to monitor and report on the performance of various components, such as the engine, transmission, and emissions systems. Additionally, the growing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) is driving innovations in OBD systems tailored for EV diagnostics.
