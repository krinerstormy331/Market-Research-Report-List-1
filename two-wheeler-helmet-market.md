<p><strong>The Two-Wheeler Helmet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full face</li><li>Half Face</li><li>Open Face</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler helmet market is categorized primarily into three types: full face, half face, and open face helmets. Full face helmets provide maximum protection, encompassing the entire head and face, ideal for high-speed riding. Half face helmets cover the top and sides of the head, offering a balance between protection and visibility, popular for casual riding. Open face helmets offer minimal coverage, exposing the face, making them preferred for city commuting where comfort and airflow are prioritized.</p></p>
