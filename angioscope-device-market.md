<p><strong>The Angioscope Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Occlusion Angioscope</li><li>Occlusion Angioscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angioscope device market is categorized into two main types: non-occlusion angioscopes and occlusion angioscopes. Non-occlusion angioscopes allow for visualization of vascular structures without obstruction, facilitating procedures where continuous blood flow is desired. In contrast, occlusion angioscopes are designed to momentarily block blood flow to enhance visibility during surgical interventions. Each type serves distinct clinical purposes, contributing to advancements in minimally invasive techniques and improving patient outcomes in cardiovascular and vascular surgeries.</p></p>
