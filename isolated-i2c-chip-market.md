<p><p>The isolated I2C chip market is categorized into SCK Mode Single Direction and SCK Mode Dual Direction. </p><p>In SCK Mode Single Direction, data transmission occurs from the master to the slave only, ensuring simpler communication and reduced complexity in design. </p><p>In contrast, SCK Mode Dual Direction allows bidirectional data flow between the master and slave, enhancing flexibility and enabling more sophisticated functionalities in applications. Both modes are crucial for maintaining data integrity in electrically noisy environments while ensuring isolation between components.</p></p>
