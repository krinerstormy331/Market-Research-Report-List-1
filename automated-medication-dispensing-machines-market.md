<p><strong>The Automated Medication Dispensing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized Dispensing Systems</li><li>Decentralized Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Medication Dispensing Machines (AMDMs) enhance medication management in healthcare settings through two main types: centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing systems operate from a central location, typically a pharmacy, where medications are stored and dispensed in bulk. In contrast, decentralized dispensing systems are situated closer to patient care areas, such as nursing stations, enabling immediate access to medications. Both systems aim to improve efficiency, reduce medication errors, and streamline the medication administration process within healthcare facilities.</p></p>
