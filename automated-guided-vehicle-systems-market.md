<p><p>Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) systems can be classified into several types based on their navigation technologies. Magnetic guided AGVs use magnetic strips or fields to navigate, offering precise route management in controlled environments. Laser guided AGVs, on the other hand, utilize laser scanning and reflectors to map their surroundings and adapt to changes in real-time, providing flexibility. Other types may include vision-guided and inertial-guided systems, which employ various sensors and algorithms for navigation, enhancing operational versatility in diverse applications.</p></p>
