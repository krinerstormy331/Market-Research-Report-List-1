<p><p>The sterile surgical yarn market is divided into two main types: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Sterile absorbable surgical yarns are designed to dissolve in the body over time, making them ideal for internal use where permanent sutures are unnecessary, which aids in wound healing. In contrast, sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns are intended for long-term use and remain in the body, providing durable support for tissues and structures requiring enduring strength.</p></p>
