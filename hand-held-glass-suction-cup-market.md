<p><p>The hand-held glass suction cup market is divided into two main types: manual suction cups and automatic suction cups. Manual suction cups require users to apply pressure to create a vacuum, making them cost-effective and easy to use for various tasks. In contrast, automatic suction cups utilize mechanical or electronic systems to create and control the suction, offering convenience and efficiency for heavier or more delicate glass handling.
