<p><p>Phased Array TR (Transmit-Receive) Chips are crucial in radar and communication systems, categorized primarily into Millimeter Wave and Centimeter Wave markets. Millimeter Wave TR chips operate typically in the 30 GHz to 300 GHz range, enabling high-resolution imaging and data transmission, essential in applications like automotive radar and 5G networks. In contrast, Centimeter Wave TR chips, operating between 1 GHz and 30 GHz, are widely used in satellite communications and wireless systems, offering longer-range capabilities with broader signal coverage. Both markets are integral to advancing modern technology.</p></p>
