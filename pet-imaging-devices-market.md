<p><p>PET imaging devices are categorized into fixed and mobile markets. Fixed PET devices are permanently installed in hospitals and imaging centers, providing high-resolution scans and accommodating larger patient volumes. These systems often feature advanced technologies and capabilities for detailed diagnostics. Mobile PET devices, on the other hand, are contained within transportable units, enabling flexibility in service delivery. They allow healthcare providers to reach remote or underserved areas, ensuring broader access to diagnostic imaging without compromising on quality.</p></p>
