<p><p>The degassing system market comprises several types designed to remove dissolved gases from liquids. Membrane degassing systems use selective permeable membranes to allow gas escape while retaining liquid. Vacuum degassing systems utilize reduced pressure to promote gas release, effectively lowering gas concentrations. Flash degassing systems apply rapid pressure reduction, causing gas to evaporate quickly. Other market segments include innovative technologies like ultrasonic degassing or chemical methods, catering to varied industrial applications such as food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
