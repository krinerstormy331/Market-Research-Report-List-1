<p><p>The bacterial identification system market encompasses various technologies utilized for identifying bacterial species in clinical and research settings. Key types include the Antibiogram, which assesses bacterial sensitivity to antibiotics; MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight), a mass spectrometry technique providing rapid bacterial identification; and Gram Staining, a traditional method distinguishing bacteria based on their cell wall properties. Each method offers unique advantages in speed, accuracy, and application, catering to diverse needs in microbiology and infection control.</p></p>
