Level 3 (L3) offers conditional automation, where vehicles can manage most driving tasks but require human intervention in certain scenarios. Level 4 (L4) represents high automation, allowing cars to operate independently in specific conditions. Level 5 (L5) signifies full autonomy, requiring no human input in any situation.</p></p>
