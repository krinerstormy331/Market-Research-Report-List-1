<p><strong>The Municipal Solid Waste Incineration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Grate</li><li>Rotary-kiln</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Municipal Solid Waste Incineration (MSWI) market comprises three primary types: Moving Grate, Rotary Kiln, and Fluidized Bed. The Moving Grate system utilizes a continuous conveyor to feed waste into the furnace, ensuring efficient combustion. The Rotary Kiln allows for better mixing and thermal treatment of waste by rotating the cylinder, which enhances heat transfer. Fluidized Bed technology employs a bed of sand or particles suspended by air, promoting uniform combustion and effectively handling various waste types, resulting in reduced emissions and ash generation.</p></p>
