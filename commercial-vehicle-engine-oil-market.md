<p><p>The commercial vehicle engine oil market comprises two primary types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil, offering basic engine protection and being cost-effective for standard applications. In contrast, synthetic oil is chemically engineered to provide superior performance, enhanced temperature stability, and better protection against wear and deposits. These oils cater to the needs of various commercial vehicles, with synthetic options increasingly favored for high-performance engines, leading to improved efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
