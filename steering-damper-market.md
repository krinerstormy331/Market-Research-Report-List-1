<p><strong>The Steering Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering damper market comprises primarily two types: rotary type and piston type. Rotary type dampers utilize a rotational motion to reduce steering vibrations and improve handling, commonly found in motorcycles and off-road vehicles. Piston type dampers, on the other hand, employ a piston mechanism within a cylinder to provide resistance against steering input, enhancing stability and control in cars and heavy machinery. Both types contribute significantly to vehicle comfort and safety, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
