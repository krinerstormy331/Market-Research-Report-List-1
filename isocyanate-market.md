<p><p>The isocyanate market features primarily two types: water-based blocked isocyanate and solvent-based blocked isocyanate. Water-based blocked isocyanate utilizes water as a solvent, offering environmental advantages and reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs). It is often favored in eco-friendly applications. Conversely, solvent-based blocked isocyanate relies on organic solvents, providing superior performance in specific applications but potentially higher VOC emissions. Both types are essential for making durable coatings, adhesives, and sealants, catering to diverse industrial needs while varying in their environmental impacts and performance characteristics.</p></p>
