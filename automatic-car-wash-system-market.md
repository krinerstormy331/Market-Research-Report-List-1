<p><p>The Automatic Car Wash System market features two primary types: the Gantry Car Wash System and the Conveyor Tunnel System. The Gantry Car Wash System operates with a stationary frame that moves over the vehicle, providing thorough cleaning with minimal space requirements. In contrast, the Conveyor Tunnel System utilizes a continuous moving conveyor belt, allowing vehicles to pass through a series of cleaning processes and brushes, ensuring efficient and high-capacity washing for commercial settings. Both systems enhance vehicle cleaning speed and effectiveness.</p></p>
