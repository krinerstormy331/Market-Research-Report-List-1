<p><p>The underdrain market includes various types used for effective water management in construction and landscaping. Flat underdrains are horizontally placed, directing water away from surfaces. Filter cover underdrains feature a filter layer to prevent clogging while draining excess water. Cone underdrains are shaped like a funnel, optimizing drainage efficiency in sloped areas. Horizontal culverts are enclosed channels facilitating water flow beneath roads or embankments, minimizing erosion and enhancing stability. Each type serves specific drainage needs in diverse applications.</p></p>
