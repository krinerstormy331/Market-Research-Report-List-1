<p><p>The A&D converters market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. </p><p>Pipeline ADCs deliver high-speed and moderate resolution, ideal for video and telecommunications. SAR ADCs (Successive Approximation Register) offer high precision with moderate speed, suitable for medical and industrial sensors. Sigma-Delta ADCs excel in low-speed, high-resolution applications like audio systems. Flash ADCs provide the fastest conversion times but are limited to lower resolutions, making them optimal for high-frequency signals. The market also includes other specialized converters for niche applications.</p></p>
