<p><p>The thermocouple strip market consists of various types, each suited for specific temperature ranges and applications. Type T (copper-constantan) is ideal for low temperatures. Type J (iron-constantan) is suitable for moderate temperatures but has limited high-temperature use. Type E (chromel-constantan) is known for its high sensitivity. Type K (chromel-alumel) is widely used due to its broad temperature range. Type N (nicrosil-nisil) offers high stability, while Type S and R (platinum-rhodium) cater to high-temperature processes in metallurgy and laboratory settings.</p></p>
