<p><p>The drainage stent market can be categorized into mental and plastic types based on material composition. Metal stents, typically made of materials like stainless steel or nitinol, offer durability and resistance to encrustation, making them suitable for long-term use in obstructed pathways. In contrast, plastic stents, commonly made from silicone or polyurethane, are more flexible and are often used for short-term applications. Both types serve to relieve blockages in various bodily systems, facilitating fluid drainage and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
