<p><p>The Vehicle Cable Market includes various types of materials used for insulation and sheathing. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is cost-effective, durable, and flame-resistant, widely used in automotive wiring. Polypropylene (PP) offers lightweight, chemical-resistant properties, suitable for high-temperature environments. Cross-linked Polyethylene (XLPE) provides excellent thermal and mechanical strength, ideal for high-voltage applications. Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) is flexible, abrasion-resistant, and used in harsh conditions. Polyphenylene Ether (PPE) offers high heat resistance and electrical insulation. Other materials cater to specialized needs, balancing performance, durability, and cost for diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
