<p><p>Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) are advanced machines designed to navigate environments independently, enhancing efficiency in various industries. The market is primarily divided into two types: LiDAR-based and LiDAR+Vision-based AMRs. LiDAR-based AMRs utilize laser technology for precise distance measurement and mapping, allowing for effective navigation. In contrast, LiDAR+Vision-based AMRs combine LiDAR with camera systems, enhancing environmental perception and object recognition, which improves decision-making capabilities and operational versatility. Both types are crucial for automation in warehousing, logistics, and manufacturing.</p></p>
