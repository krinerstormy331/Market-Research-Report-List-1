<p><p>The geared motors market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Worm type geared motors offer high gear reduction and compact design, making them ideal for applications needing high torque. Planetary type motors provide efficient power transmission and compactness, commonly used in robotics and automotive sectors. Helical type geared motors excel in smooth operation and high efficiency, suitable for various industrial applications. Helical bevel type combines helical gears and bevel gears, enabling angular motion and versatility in space-constrained settings.</p></p>
