<p><p>The cyclocomputer market consists of three main types: wired computers, wireless computers, and wireless GPS computers. Wired computers connect directly to the bike's frame using cables, offering reliability at lower costs. Wireless computers use Bluetooth or ANT+ technology to transmit data without cables, providing cleaner aesthetics and easier installation. Wireless GPS computers combine GPS tracking with cycling metrics, offering advanced navigation and route mapping, catering to cyclists who seek performance analysis and outdoor navigation features.</p></p>
