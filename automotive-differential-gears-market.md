<p><strong>The Automotive Differential Gears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypoid Differential Gears</li><li>Spiral Differential Gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive differential gears market includes various types, such as hypoid and spiral differential gears. Hypoid differential gears feature a unique design where the pinion gear is offset from the centerline of the ring gear, enabling smoother and quieter operation with higher torque capacity, commonly used in rear-wheel-drive vehicles. Spiral differential gears use curved teeth for improved contact and load distribution, enhancing durability and efficiency, often found in high-performance and heavy-duty applications. Both types optimize power transmission and vehicle performance.</p></p>
