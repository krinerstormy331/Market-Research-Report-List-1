<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask inspection equipment is crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, employing two primary market methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects each die by comparing it to adjacent dies on the same mask, ensuring uniformity and defect detection in a batch. Conversely, the DB method involves comparing the mask's features against a comprehensive database of known good patterns, enabling identification of discrepancies and improving overall mask quality. Both methods enhance yield and reliability in production.</p></p>
