<p><p>The UV Indicator Card market is segmented based on the type of ultraviolet radiation it measures: UVA, UVB, UVC, and UVD. UVA cards detect long-wave UV radiation, primarily linked to skin aging and tanning. UVB cards measure medium-wave UV, associated with sunburn and skin cancer risks. UVC cards are designed for short-wave UV radiation, often used in sterilization processes. UVD cards, focused on a broader spectrum, help monitor overall UV exposure, crucial for safety in various environments.</p></p>
