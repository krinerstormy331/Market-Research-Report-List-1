<p><strong>The Bus Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus tire market primarily consists of two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with crisscrossed layers of rubber-coated fabric plies, offering durability and load-bearing capacity, making them suitable for rough terrains. Radial tires feature steel belts and perpendicular ply layers, providing better fuel efficiency, heat resistance, and smoother rides, ideal for long-distance travel. While bias tires are cost-effective and robust, radial tires dominate the market due to their performance advantages, aligning with modern transportation demands.</p></p>
