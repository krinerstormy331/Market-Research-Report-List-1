<p><p>The disposable tourniquet market consists primarily of latex and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditional and provide excellent elasticity and strength, making them widely used in medical settings. However, they can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, are latex-free, making them safer for allergy-prone patients. They offer similar flexibility and durability, appealing to modern medical practices emphasizing safety and patient comfort. Both types are crucial for effective blood flow control during medical procedures.</p></p>
