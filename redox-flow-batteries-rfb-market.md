<p><p>Redox Flow Batteries (RFB) market primarily comprises two types: Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries and Hybrid Flow Batteries. Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries utilize vanadium ions in different oxidation states to store energy, offering high efficiency and scalability for large-scale applications. In contrast, Hybrid Flow Batteries combine elements of traditional batteries, such as lithium-ion, with flow technology, enhancing energy density and versatility. Both types cater to energy storage demands in renewable energy integration, grid balancing, and off-grid applications, promoting sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
