<p><p>The e-liquids market consists of three main types: PG base, VG base, and blended PG/VG e-liquids. PG (propylene glycol) base e-liquids are known for delivering a stronger throat hit and better flavor carry, making them popular among former smokers. VG (vegetable glycerin) base e-liquids provide a smoother vaping experience with thicker vapor production but less intense flavor. Blended PG/VG e-liquids combine both bases, balancing throat hit, flavor, and vapor production, catering to diverse preferences among vapers.</p></p>
