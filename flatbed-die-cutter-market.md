<p><p>A flatbed die-cutter is a machine used in the packaging and printing industries to cut, crease, and shape materials such as paper, cardboard, and corrugated board into precise designs. It operates using a flatbed system where a die, customized to the desired shape, presses against the material to create the final product. Flatbed die-cutters are widely used for their accuracy, versatility, and ability to handle complex designs, making them essential for industries like e-commerce, food packaging, and consumer goods.</p><p>The Flatbed Die-Cutter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period. Overall, the flatbed die-cutter market is poised for steady expansion, supported by technological innovation and evolving industry needs.</p></p>
