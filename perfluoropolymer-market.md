<p><p>The perfluoropolymer market includes various types of polymers, predominantly PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene), and PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy). PTFE is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it ideal for coatings and insulation. FEP offers similar properties but with enhanced transparency and flexibility, suitable for wiring and tubing applications. PFA combines the advantageous features of both PTFE and FEP, providing greater processing versatility, which is essential in chemical handling and food processing industries.</p></p>
