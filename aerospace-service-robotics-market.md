<p><p>The aerospace service robotics market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Articulated robots utilize jointed arms for versatile movement, ideal for assembly tasks. Cylindrical robots operate within a cylindrical work envelope, optimizing space for welding and material handling. SCARA robots excel in high-speed, precision tasks, making them perfect for assembly lines. Cartesian robots, with linear movements along three axes, are effective for pick-and-place operations. Other robots may include delta or collaborative types, enhancing flexibility in various aerospace applications.</p></p>
