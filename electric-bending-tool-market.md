<p><p>The Electric Bending Tool market can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual tools require the operator to exert physical effort to bend materials, offering flexibility but demanding skill and time. Semi-automatic tools streamline the process with some powered assistance, enhancing efficiency while still requiring operator input. Automatic tools fully automate the bending process, increasing production speed and consistency, reducing labor costs, and minimizing the risk of human error, making them ideal for high-volume applications.</p></p>
