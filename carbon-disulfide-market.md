<p><p>The Carbon Disulfide (CS₂) market is segmented into Natural Gas Method and Coke Method. The Natural Gas Method involves reacting methane with sulfur, offering higher purity and lower environmental impact, making it preferred for industrial applications. The Coke Method uses coke and sulfur, which is cost-effective but less efficient and more polluting. Both methods cater to industries like rubber, textiles, and chemicals, with the Natural Gas Method gaining traction due to sustainability trends and stricter environmental regulations.</p></p>
