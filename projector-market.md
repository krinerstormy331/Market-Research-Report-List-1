DLP projectors utilize micro-mirror technology for clear and dynamic visuals, often preferred for high-quality video playback. LED projectors leverage light-emitting diodes for longer lifespans and energy efficiency, ideal for portable uses. Other types include laser and hybrid projectors, catering to specialized needs across various applications.</p></p>
