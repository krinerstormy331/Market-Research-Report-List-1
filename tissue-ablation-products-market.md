<p><p>The tissue ablation products market encompasses various technologies designed to remove or destroy tissue. Photonic and laser-based systems utilize focused light to achieve precise ablation, often used in dermatology and oncology. Microwave ablation systems employ heat generated from microwaves for effective tissue destruction, primarily in tumor treatment. Radiation therapy systems deliver high-energy radiation to target and obliterate cancer cells. Cryotherapy systems utilize extreme cold to induce tissue necrosis. Other methods may include radiofrequency ablation and chemical agents, expanding treatment options in medicine.</p></p>
