<p><strong>The Wall-mounted Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way Flow Type</li><li>Bidirectional Flow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wall-mounted ventilator market is categorized into two primary types: one-way flow type and bidirectional flow type. One-way flow ventilators facilitate unidirectional airflow, typically exhausting indoor air outside, thereby promoting fresh air circulation. In contrast, bidirectional flow ventilators enable simultaneous intake of fresh air and exhaust of stale air, enhancing indoor air quality. Both types are used in various settings, such as healthcare facilities and residential spaces, to ensure efficient ventilation and improved air quality.</p></p>
