<p><p>Inventory management systems are categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and on-demand/cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed locally on a company's hardware, providing full control over data and customization but often requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, on-demand or cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and lower initial costs, making them suitable for businesses seeking flexibility and easier accessibility in managing inventory.</p></p>
