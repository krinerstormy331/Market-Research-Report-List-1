<p><p>The digital temperature gauge market features various types, including bimetal and gas-actuated temperature gauges. Bimetal temperature gauges utilize two bonded metal strips that expand at different rates, providing accurate readings through a mechanical dial. Gas-actuated temperature gauges, on the other hand, use gas-filled sensing elements that respond to temperature changes, allowing for precise digital output. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring temperature monitoring and control in environments such as manufacturing, HVAC, and automotive industries.</p></p>
