<p><p>Aramid Fiber Reinforced Plastic (AFRP) is a composite material combining aramid fibers with plastic matrices, offering high strength, lightweight, and durability. The market is divided into **Thermoplastic** and **Thermoset** types. **Thermoplastic AFRP** uses polymers that soften when heated and harden when cooled, allowing for recyclability and reshaping. **Thermoset AFRP** employs polymers that irreversibly harden during curing, providing superior heat resistance and structural stability. Both types cater to industries like aerospace, automotive, and defense, with thermoplastics favored for flexibility and thermosets for high-performance applications.</p></p>
