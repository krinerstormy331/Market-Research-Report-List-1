<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are critical components in dry etching equipment used in semiconductor manufacturing. The Coulomb type operates by generating an electrostatic field to hold wafers securely during processing through attractive forces. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type utilizes a combination of electrostatic and mechanical interactions, allowing for precise wafer positioning and improved thermal management. Both types enhance etching efficiency, reduce contamination risks, and improve process uniformity, catering to the demands of advanced semiconductor fabrication technologies.</p></p>
