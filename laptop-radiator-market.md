<p><strong>The Laptop Radiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Laptop Radiator</li><li>Passive Laptop Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laptop radiator market is segmented into two main types: active and passive laptop radiators. Active laptop radiators use fans or liquid cooling systems to enhance airflow and dissipate heat efficiently, making them ideal for high-performance laptops. In contrast, passive laptop radiators operate without mechanical components, relying on heat dissipation through materials like aluminum or copper to manage temperature. Both types aim to improve thermal performance, prolong device lifespan, and provide a better user experience during intensive tasks.</p></p>
