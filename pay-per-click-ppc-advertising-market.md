<p><strong>The Pay-per-click (PPC) Advertising Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat-rate PPC</li><li>Bid-based PPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising encompasses two primary market types: flat-rate PPC and bid-based PPC. In a flat-rate PPC model, advertisers pay a fixed amount per click, irrespective of competition, which provides budget predictability. Conversely, bid-based PPC involves a competitive auction system where advertisers set a maximum bid for clicks, with costs fluctuating based on demand and competition for specific keywords. This dynamic approach can lead to varying costs while influencing ad placement and visibility based on bid amounts.</p></p>
