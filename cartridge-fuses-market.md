<p><p>Cartridge fuses are essential components in electrical systems, categorized into three main types. Fast blow fuses provide immediate protection against surges, ideal for sensitive electronics. Medium or normal blow fuses balance between quick response and overload tolerance, suitable for general applications. Time delay or slow blow fuses withstand temporary overloads, making them ideal for equipment with high inrush currents, such as motors. Each type serves specific needs based on the application's electrical characteristics and protection requirements.</p></p>
