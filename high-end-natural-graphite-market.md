<p><ul><li>Amorphous Graphite</li><li>Flake Graphite</li><li>Crystalline Vein Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end natural graphite market comprises three main types: amorphous, flake, and crystalline vein graphite. Amorphous graphite features a non-crystalline structure, offering versatility in applications but lower purity. Flake graphite, characterized by its layered structure, boasts higher purity and is sought after in batteries and lubricants. Crystalline vein graphite, the rarest, possesses the highest purity and is ideal for high-performance applications. Together, these types cater to diverse industries, including technology, energy, and manufacturing, driven by the demand for advanced materials.</p></p>
