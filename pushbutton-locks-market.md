<p><p>The pushbutton locks market is divided into two main types: electronic pushbutton locks and mechanical pushbutton locks. Electronic pushbutton locks utilize digital technology, offering features like keypad entry, programmable codes, and integration with smart home systems for enhanced security and convenience. While both types provide keyless entry solutions, electronic variants typically offer advanced functionalities, while mechanical locks are simpler and often more reliable in extreme conditions.</p></p>
