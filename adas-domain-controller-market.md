<p><p>ADAS Domain Controllers are categorized into three market types based on their automation levels. </p><p>1. **Below L2 Domain Controller:** These systems support basic driver assistance features but do not provide any automatic driving capability, focusing on functions like adaptive cruise control.</p><p>2. **L2-L3 Domain Controller:** This category encompasses advanced systems enabling partial automation, allowing the vehicle to handle specific driving tasks while still requiring driver supervision.</p><p>3. **Above L3 Domain Controller:** These controllers facilitate higher automation levels, enabling vehicles to operate independently in certain conditions with minimal driver intervention, pivotal for future autonomous driving technologies.</p></p>
