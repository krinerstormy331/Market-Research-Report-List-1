<p><strong>The Portable Water Penetration Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Pressure Difference Method</li><li>Static Pressure Head Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Water Penetration Testers market includes two primary types: the Air Pressure Difference Method and the Static Pressure Head Method. The Air Pressure Difference Method assesses water resistance by creating a pressure differential, allowing for the evaluation of potential water ingress in building materials. Conversely, the Static Pressure Head Method measures water penetration by applying a static water column, providing insights into the material's ability to withstand hydrostatic pressure. Both methods are essential for ensuring durability and water-tightness in construction.</p></p>
