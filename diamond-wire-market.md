<p><p>The Diamond Wire Market is primarily categorized into two types: Electroplated Diamond Wire and Resin Diamond Wire. Electroplated Diamond Wire features a metal bond that securely holds diamond particles, providing high precision and durability for cutting hard materials like granite and concrete. In contrast, Resin Diamond Wire utilizes a resin bond, offering flexibility and smooth cutting with less waste, making it ideal for applications like solar cell manufacturing and marble processing. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, ensuring effective material cutting.</p></p>
