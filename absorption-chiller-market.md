<p><p>The absorption chiller market is categorized into three main types: single effect, double effect, and triple effect absorption chillers. Single effect chillers offer basic cooling efficiency and are typically used in smaller applications. Double effect chillers improve efficiency by utilizing two heat sources, making them suitable for larger, industrial applications. Triple effect chillers further enhance efficiency, allowing for higher cooling capacities and reduced energy consumption, making them ideal for energy-intensive industries where cost savings and sustainability are priorities.</p></p>
