<p><p>Plant hydrosols, also known as floral waters or distillates, are aromatic waters produced during the steam distillation of plant materials, such as flowers, leaves, or herbs. Hydrosols are valued for their mild nature, making them suitable for sensitive skin and holistic wellness applications.</p><p>The Plant Hydrosol Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer demand for natural and organic products.
