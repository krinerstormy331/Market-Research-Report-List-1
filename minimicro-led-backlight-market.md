<p><p>The MiniMicro LED backlight market encompasses two key technologies: Mini-LED and MicroLED. MicroLED, on the other hand, consists of individual pixels made of microscopic LEDs, offering self-emissive properties that deliver exceptional brightness, color accuracy, and energy efficiency.
