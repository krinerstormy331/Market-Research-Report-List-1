<p><p>The athlete's foot (Tinea pedis) treatment market is categorized into three main types: interdigital, erosion type, and blister type. Interdigital treatment focuses on the spaces between the toes, targeting fungal infections that thrive in these moist areas. Erosion type treatment addresses skin erosion and inflammation caused by more severe fungal infections. Blister type treatment is aimed at managing fluid-filled blisters resulting from the infection. Each category requires specific antifungal agents and management approaches to effectively alleviate symptoms and promote healing.</p></p>
