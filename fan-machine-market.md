<p><p>The market for residential fans is divided primarily into AC and DC fans. AC residential fans, powered by alternating current, are widely used for their affordability and reliability, offering efficient cooling solutions. In contrast, DC residential fans utilize direct current, providing enhanced energy efficiency and quieter operation, often featuring variable speed settings. As energy efficiency becomes increasingly important, the demand for DC fans is growing, while AC fans remain popular for traditional use due to their lower initial cost.</p></p>
