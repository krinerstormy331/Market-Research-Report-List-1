<p><p>Air-Operated Double-Diaphragm (AODD) pumps are versatile and efficient, utilizing compressed air to move fluids. In the industrial market, two primary materials are used: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) and metal. PTFE materials are known for their chemical resistance and low friction properties, making them ideal for corrosive applications. Conversely, metal materials, often aluminum or stainless steel, offer durability and strength, suitable for handling abrasive or high-pressure fluids. This combination caters to diverse industries, ensuring optimal performance across various applications.</p></p>
