<p><strong>The Metallic Craft Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic craft paint is available in two primary types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based paints use chemical solvents as carriers, offering durability, fast drying, and a high-gloss finish, but emit strong fumes and require proper ventilation. Water-based paints use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendliness, low odor, and easy cleanup, though they may dry slower and require multiple coats. Both types are popular in crafting, with solvent-based preferred for industrial applications and water-based favored for home use due to safety and environmental benefits.</p></p>
