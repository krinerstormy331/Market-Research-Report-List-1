<p><p>The ski helmet market primarily features two types of shells: PC (polycarbonate) and ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene). PC shells are lightweight and provide excellent impact resistance, making them popular among serious skiers. They offer better durability and flexibility but can be more expensive. In contrast, ABS shells are sturdier and more affordable, providing reliable protection, though they are slightly heavier. Both types cater to varying preferences and budgets, ensuring safety and comfort for skiers of all levels.</p></p>
