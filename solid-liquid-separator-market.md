<p><p>The solid-liquid separator market is categorized primarily into three types: screw separators, disc stack separators, and others. Screw separators utilize a rotating screw mechanism to separate solids from liquids, making them effective for thicker slurries. Disc stack separators employ a series of discs to create a high centrifugal force, efficiently separating fine particles from liquids, ideal for wastewater treatment and food processing. Other separators may include various technologies like belt or pressure filters, catering to niche applications across industries.</p></p>
