<p><p>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) 3D printing is an additive manufacturing technology that uses a laser to fuse powdered materials, predominantly plastic, into solid structures. This process allows for the creation of complex geometries and durable prototypes, making it suitable for various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and healthcare. The SLS process is characterized by its ability to produce high-strength parts without the need for support structures, as the unsintered powder acts as a support medium.</p><p>The SLS 3D Printing Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 9.50% during the forecast period.
