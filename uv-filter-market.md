Additionally, the cosmetics and personal care industry is witnessing a surge in demand for multifunctional products with UV protection, further boosting market growth.</p><p>Emerging trends in the UV filter market include the development of eco-friendly and biodegradable filters, as consumers increasingly prioritize sustainability. There is also a rising demand for broad-spectrum UV filters that protect against both UVA and UVB rays. The Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the market due to its large population, increasing disposable income, and growing awareness of skincare. Overall, the UV filter market is poised for steady growth, driven by innovation and consumer demand for effective sun protection solutions.</p></p>
