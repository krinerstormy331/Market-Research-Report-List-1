<p><p>The PERC solar cell market is divided into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. In contrast, double-sided PERC cells, also known as Bifacial cells, can capture sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy production by utilizing reflected light from the ground. This dual absorption capability often leads to higher efficiency and increased overall energy yield, appealing to various commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
