<p><p>Surfactants used in Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) are classified mainly into anionic surfactants and other types. Anionic surfactants possess a negatively charged hydrophilic head, which enhances oil displacement by reducing surface tension and improving wettability. Other surfactants include cationic, non-ionic, and amphoteric variants, each optimized for specific reservoir conditions and oil characteristics. Together, these surfactants enhance the efficiency of oil extraction processes, improving yield while minimizing environmental impact during oil recovery efforts.</p></p>
