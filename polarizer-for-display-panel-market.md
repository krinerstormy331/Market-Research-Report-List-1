<p><p>The polarizer market for display panels includes various types such as TFT, TN, and STN polarizers. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) polarizers are used in high-resolution displays, providing better contrast and color accuracy. TN (Twisted Nematic) polarizers are found in cost-effective displays, offering faster response times but limited viewing angles. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) polarizers enhance color depth and improve viewing angles compared to TN, making them suitable for low-power applications. Each type serves distinct display technology needs, driving market segmentation.</p></p>
