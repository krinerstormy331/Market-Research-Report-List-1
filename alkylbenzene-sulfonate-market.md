<p><p>The Alkylbenzene Sulfonate market is primarily divided into two types: Linear Alkylbenzene Sulfonate (LAS) and Branched Alkylbenzene Sulfonate (BAS). LAS is produced using linear alkylbenzene, offering higher biodegradability and effectiveness in detergents, making it popular for consumer cleaning products. In contrast, BAS is derived from branched alkylbenzene, which, while less biodegradable, is often less expensive and effective in specific industrial applications. These distinctions influence their usage in household and industrial cleaning formulations, impacting environmental considerations and pricing strategies.</p></p>
