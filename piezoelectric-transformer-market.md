<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step-up Transformer</li><li>Step-down Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric transformer market is primarily divided into step-up and step-down transformers. Step-up transformers increase voltage while maintaining low current, making them suitable for applications like energy harvesting and ignition systems. In contrast, step-down transformers reduce voltage to ensure safe operation in consumer electronics, medical devices, and automotive applications. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials for efficient energy conversion, offering advantages such as compact size, high efficiency, and minimal electromagnetic interference, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
