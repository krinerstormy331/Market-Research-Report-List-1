<p><ul><li>Negative Type</li><li>Positive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconducting glass market is primarily divided into two types: negative-type and positive-type semiconducting glasses. Negative-type semiconducting glasses exhibit an increase in resistance when exposed to light, making them suitable for applications like solar cells and photodetectors. In contrast, positive-type semiconducting glasses show a decrease in resistance under light exposure, facilitating their use in optoelectronic devices and sensors. These distinct characteristics cater to various industries, enhancing the functionality and efficiency of electronic components.</p></p>
