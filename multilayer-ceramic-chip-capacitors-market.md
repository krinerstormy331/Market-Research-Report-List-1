<p><p>The multilayer ceramic chip capacitors (MLCC) market includes various types, each with distinct properties. X7R capacitors offer high capacitance and stability across temperature ranges, ideal for energy storage. X5R capacitors provide moderate capacitance with good temperature performance, suitable for decoupling applications. C0G (NP0) capacitors deliver excellent stability and low loss, making them perfect for precision circuits. Y5V capacitors have high capacitance but low stability, used in less critical applications. Other types include alternatives like C0G and niche ceramics for specialized needs.</p></p>
