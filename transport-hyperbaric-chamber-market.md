<p><p>The transport hyperbaric chamber market includes two primary types: air-pressured and oxygen-pressured hyperbaric chambers. Air-pressured hyperbaric chambers use ambient air to create pressure, making them suitable for various applications, including decompression therapy. In contrast, oxygen-pressured hyperbaric chambers utilize pure oxygen at higher pressures, primarily for medical treatments such as promoting healing and combating infections. Both types are designed to be portable and facilitate the effective delivery of hyperbaric therapy in emergencies and remote locations.</p></p>
