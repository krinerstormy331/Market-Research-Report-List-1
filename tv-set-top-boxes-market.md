<p><p>The TV set-top box market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Cable** boxes connect to cable networks, offering a range of channels and on-demand content.</p><p>2. **Satellite** boxes receive signals from satellites, providing extensive programming options in remote areas.</p><p>3. **DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television)** boxes decode digital signals over the air, popular for free local channels.</p><p>4. **IP (Internet Protocol)** boxes use broadband connections to stream content directly from the internet.</p><p>5. **OTT (Over-The-Top)** boxes deliver streaming services bypassing traditional distribution, focusing on subscription-based models.</p></p>
