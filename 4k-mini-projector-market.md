<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>DLP</li><li>LCOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4K mini projector market consists of three primary types: LCD, DLP, and LCoS. </p><p>LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to produce bright and vibrant images, ideal for well-lit environments. DLP projectors utilize a digital light processing chip, providing sharp images and faster response times, making them suitable for dynamic content. </p><p>LCoS projectors combine liquid crystal technology with reflective surfaces, resulting in high resolution and excellent color accuracy, often preferred for home theater setups. Each type caters to different user preferences and viewing conditions.</p></p>
