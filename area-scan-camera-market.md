<p><strong>The Area Scan Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The area scan camera market is primarily divided into two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors. CMOS cameras are known for their lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for various applications such as industrial automation and consumer electronics. In contrast, CCD cameras deliver superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for medical imaging and scientific research. Each type serves distinct needs within the imaging and surveillance sectors.</p></p>
