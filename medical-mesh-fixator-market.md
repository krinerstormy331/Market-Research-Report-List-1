<p><strong>The Medical Mesh Fixator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tack Applicators</li><li>Glue Applicators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Mesh Fixator market comprises primarily two types: Tack Applicators and Glue Applicators. Tack Applicators utilize specialized devices to securely affix mesh to tissue, ensuring stability during healing. These are often favored for their precision and ease of use in surgical settings. Glue Applicators, on the other hand, employ adhesive substances to bond the mesh to surrounding tissues, providing an alternative method for securing implants. Both types enhance the effectiveness of mesh applications in various medical procedures, particularly in hernia repairs and wound management.</p></p>
